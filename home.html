Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, our team will review your medical history, perform a full dental exam, and provide recommendations for treatment if necessary.

Do you offer emergency dental care?

Yes, we offer emergency dental care for all of our patients. You can contact us anytime in case of dental emergencies.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as insurance plans. You can also inquire about flexible payment plans.

How often should I schedule dental cleanings?

We recommend scheduling a cleaning and check-up every six months to ensure your teeth and gums stay healthy.

Do you provide services for children?

Yes, we provide a full range of pediatric dental services for children of all ages, from routine check-ups to special treatments.

What should I do if I have a dental emergency?

If you have a dental emergency, such as a knocked-out tooth or severe pain, contact us immediately. We prioritize emergency cases.
